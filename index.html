Language, Identity & Pride

21st February, 1952 is an unforgettable and glorious day for the Bengali nation and all Bangla-speaking people. On this day, the brave students and general people of then East Bengal took to the streets to protect their mother tongue, Bangla, standing firmly against injustice and conspiracy.

Their sacrifice not only safeguards the Bangla language but also teaches the world that language is the foundation of a nation's identity, culture, and existence. Today, 21st February is not only significant for Bengalis, but also for people of all languages around the world.

We have created this video as a humble tribute to the Language Martyrs, expressing our deep respect and gratitude. Their sacrifice is our inspiration and our pride.

Our Story

About Us

01 Prepway Bangladesh

Prepway Bangladesh was established on August 9, 2025 — born from a simple but urgent question: why are millions of Bangladeshi students navigating SSC, HSC, and university admissions completely alone, without honest, accessible guidance?

The answer came from an unexpected place. After the first episode of the Prepway Podcast — where Afrahim Siddiqui Ahon hosted a discussion on studying abroad without IELTS or SAT — the response from students was overwhelming. The messages that flooded in were not only about studying overseas. Students were asking about local struggles too: admission test preparation, subject guidance, life after HSC. The need was right there, undeniable.

Ahon raised the idea with the other directors of Prepway Abroad. Together, they decided to build something specifically for Bangladesh — a platform that would guide students through every stage of their academic journey within the country. Prepway Bangladesh was the result. It began through podcasts, because the team believed the most powerful form of guidance was hearing it directly from students who had already been through it. Show Your Voice became that series — unscripted, real, and built entirely for Bangladesh.

02 Prepway Abroad

Before Prepway Bangladesh, before Prepway Group — there was Prepway Abroad. Founded on June 26, 2025, it was an independent initiative created by four people who shared one belief: that Bangladeshi students dreaming of international education deserved better access to honest guidance and real information.

Prepway Abroad was built as a podcast and content platform — covering real stories of Bangladeshi students studying in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and beyond. It tackled scholarships, visa processes, university applications, IELTS preparation, and the realities of student life abroad. The founding team was:

K. M. Rubaid Ferdous Swapnil
Zinat Hossain Any
Afrahim Siddiqui Ahon
Md. Emran Hasan Emon

At the time of its founding, Prepway Abroad stood alone — there was no group, no umbrella organisation. It was simply four people with a mission. That mission resonated, and the podcast grew. The response from students is ultimately what sparked the idea for Prepway Bangladesh.

03 Prepway Group

Once Prepway Bangladesh was established in August 2025, it became clear that two growing initiatives needed a shared identity, shared values, and shared direction. Prepway Group was formed as the parent organisation — an umbrella that formally brought Prepway Bangladesh and Prepway Abroad together under one roof.

Prepway Group was never the starting point. It was the natural result of growth — two platforms, one vision. Today, the group is committed to one purpose: empowering every Bangladeshi student, whether they are preparing for their HSC exam at home or applying to universities on the other side of the world.
