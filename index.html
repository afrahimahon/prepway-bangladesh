Our Story

About Us

01 Prepway Bangladesh Est. Aug 9, 2025

Born from one urgent question — why are millions of Bangladeshi students navigating SSC, HSC, and university admissions completely alone?

The answer came after the first episode of the Prepway Podcast, where Afrahim Siddiqui Ahon hosted a discussion on studying abroad. Students flooded in with local struggles too: admission prep, subject guidance, life after HSC.

Together they built Prepway Bangladesh — a platform for every stage of a student's academic journey within the country. Show Your Voice became that podcast series — unscripted, real, built entirely for Bangladesh.

02 Prepway Abroad Est. Jun 26, 2025

Before Prepway Bangladesh, before Prepway Group — four people started independently to help Bangladeshi students reach the world.

Prepway Abroad covers real student stories from the USA, UK, Canada, Australia — scholarships, visas, applications, IELTS, and life abroad. Founding team:

K. M. Rubaid Ferdous Swapnil
Zinat Hossain Any
Afrahim Siddiqui Ahon
Md. Emran Hasan Emon

Their mission resonated, grew, and ultimately sparked the idea for Prepway Bangladesh.

03 Prepway Group Est. Aug 2025

Two growing initiatives. One shared vision. Prepway Group was the natural result of growth — not the starting point.

Once Prepway Bangladesh launched, it became clear both initiatives needed a shared identity. Prepway Group was formed as the parent organisation — bringing Prepway Bangladesh and Prepway Abroad under one roof.

One purpose: empowering every Bangladeshi student, whether preparing for HSC at home or applying to universities on the other side of the world.
