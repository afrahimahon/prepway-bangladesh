# Prepway Bangladesh
### From Bangladesh, To Bangladesh — বাংলাদেশ থেকে, বাংলাদেশের জন্য

**Prepway Bangladesh** is an educational podcast and content platform dedicated to guiding Bangladeshi students through SSC, HSC, and university admissions — through real stories, honest conversations, and practical guidance.

🌐 **Live at [prepwaybd.vercel.app](https://prepwaybd.vercel.app)**

---

## What is Prepway Bangladesh?

Founded on **August 9, 2025**, Prepway Bangladesh was born from a simple realisation — millions of Bangladeshi students navigate their academic journey without honest, accessible guidance. We built this platform to change that.

We started with podcasts. Because the most powerful form of guidance is hearing it directly from students who have already been through it.

---

## Show Your Voice — Prepway Podcast

Our podcast series **Show Your Voice** features unscripted, long-form conversations with Bangladeshi students who share their real journeys — medical admissions, university life, career paths, and everything in between.

**Season 01 is live on YouTube.**
